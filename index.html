About Me

I am a hard worker with an easy-going personality who enjoys being part of a team and collaborating on projects. I am serious about my work but always keep a sense of humor about myself. I have quite a varied background and work history. I did very well in High School but skipped college to enter the trades as a Marine Pipefitter apprentice at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard. A year after graduating I was laid off and began pursuing music seriously. While pursuing music I took a job at Merrill Lynch working in their 401k Fullfillment Center. Soon I moved into managing the department. About a year later our entire office was closed and I was again layed off. Luckily a full time music opportunity presented itself at the righ time. For the next 10 years I worked producing jingles and custom music for television and radio with IQ Beats and Big Idea Music. After 10 years doing well in the industry I could see that it was becoming oversaturated with producers and devalued. That's when I began looking at a career change and ultimately decided to learn computer programming. That and a friend's recommendation is what lead me to Epicodus.

Other interests and skills

  1. Graphic design - I have many years of experience using Adobe Creative Suite products including classes on Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign at the School of Visual Concepts and Seattle Central Community College.

  4. Pugs - My girlfriend Sara and I enjoy our 2 pugs Gary (4 years old) and Agnes (2 and 1/2 years old). They are endlessly entertaining and affectionate.
  5. Brewing - I enjoy being a part-time assistant brewer at Outlander Brewing in Fremont as well as crafting some creative and fun home brews usually named after my pugs like "Agnes Abbey Ale" and "Pug Vader Black IPA".
